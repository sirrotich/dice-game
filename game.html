The dice game

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Player 1 and player 2 rolls sides 2 - 6, the dice answer is added to their total and continue rolling.
  2. when player 1 or player 2 rolls a 1, their total score reverts to 0 and it becomes the next player's turn.
  3. When the player chooses to "pass", their total is added to their total score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  4. The player who first scores 100 wins the game
